Services Offered

Community health nursing offers a variety of services aimed at improving the overall health and well-being of the community.

  1. Home
  2. Services Offered

Health Promotion and Education

Educating the community on healthy lifestyle choices, disease prevention, and health maintenance. This can include workshops, seminars, and distribution of educational materials.

Preventive Care

Administering vaccines, conducting screenings (e.g., for hypertension, diabetes, and cancer), and offering counseling on preventive measures.

Maternal and Child Health Services

Providing prenatal and postnatal care, infant and child health assessments, and education on breastfeeding, nutrition, and family planning.

Chronic Disease Management

Helping individuals manage chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and asthma through education, monitoring, and support.

Home Health Care

Offering medical care and support services in the home for those who are chronically ill, disabled, or recovering from surgery.

Mental Health Services

Providing counseling, support groups, and referrals to mental health professionals for individuals dealing with mental health issues.

MATERNAL AND CHILD HEALTH SERVICES

Maternal health is the health of women during pregnancy, childbirth, and the postpartum period. In most cases, maternal health encompasses the health care dimensions of family planning, preconception, prenatal, and postnatal care in order to ensure a positive and fulfilling experience.
